Welcome

We would love to invite you, your family, your church, and your friends to join us in Shepton Mallet, for six days of gathering to encounter the Holy Spirit across all ages and to be renewed to play our part in Local Churches Changing Nations. Encountering Jesus, spending time with friends, and having a lot of fun - what's not to love?

Come and Join Us

An exciting summer awaits! There's both fun and learning for all ages. And did ypu know that New Wine hosts more Christian organisations than any other festival? Come and explore the awesome missions being undertaken in our country and beyond

Worship

Learn more about worship this summer. Joining in song with thousands of others, led by dedicated musicians, is a unique experience. Learn new music alongside singing classics, whilst feeling God's presence.

Community

Discover Fellowship - all year round. New Wine is more than just an event It’s a network that spans across many denominations in 14 countries. This network is made up of over 1,000 churches, 4,000 church leaders, and 50,000 individuals who share a common vision of local churches changing nations.

Join us this summer to be refreshed, inspired and equipped. Whether you’re a solo traveller or coming with family, you’re welcomed to commune with the family of the UK Church to enjoy summer fun alongside transformational teaching and worship music. There’s also fun for all ages, including creche for the smallest attendees, lively activities for older children, pub quizzes for over 18s, and much more. It’s a perfect opportunity to network and fellowship with Christians from around the country - and to spend time with your own congregation. Missions, ministries and more are on hand to explore, alongside art to view and crafts to try out. Learn from some of the best Bible teachers and speakers, in Bible-studies and seminars covering both timeless and timely topics, and leave uplifted and with greater wisdom. It's an ideal option for your summer break, but it's so much more than a holiday.
